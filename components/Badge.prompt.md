Small brand label / category chip; use for slide tags, section markers, and metadata.

```jsx
<Badge variant="chip">карусель · n.01</Badge>
<Badge variant="bracket">strategy & growth</Badge>
<Badge variant="outline" tone="dark">CEO</Badge>
```

Variants: `chip` (solid sage, uppercase, wide tracking), `bracket` ([label] in body type), `outline` (hairline pill). `tone` sets color for bracket/outline on dark vs light backgrounds.
